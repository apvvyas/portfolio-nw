About Me

As a Lead Engineer (Full Stack) at Technoidentity since April 2022, I bring 9 years of experience in development of web and mobile products. I'm capable in MERN stack and work with adaptability in Agile, Waterfall, and Test-Driven Development methodologies.

I specialize in full-stack development with deep expertise in Python (Django), PHP (Laravel, CodeIgniter, Zend), modern JavaScript frameworks (React, React Native, Angular, VueJS, TypeScript), Ruby on Rails, and CMS platforms (WordPress, PrestaShop, OpenCart, October CMS). My experience spans distributed systems, microservices architecture, Docker, REST APIs, GraphQL, SOAP services, and cloud infrastructure (AWS ECS, SQS, SES, SNS). I also have hands-on experience building RAG (Retrieval-Augmented Generation) applications and Temporal workflow systems with comprehensive server management.

Throughout my career at organizations like Select Source International (where I developed recruitment platforms with WebRTC, Twilio, and personality assessment tools), Chapter247 Infotech, Kraff Softwares, and Yugtia Technologies, I've mentored development teams, architected scalable systems, and delivered products that reduce service turnaround time. I excel at software system analysis, product planning, building full server-side components with integration of payment gateways (Stripe, PayPal, PayuMoney), and implementing AI-powered solutions.

I hold a Master's degree in Software Systems from BITS Pilani (2018) and a Bachelor of Engineering from Indus Institute of Technology (graduated with high honors, 2014). I'm certified in React.js, PHP, Laravel, Django, and JLPT N-5 (Japanese Language). Currently pursuing PMP Certification through Simplilearn to enhance my project management and digital leadership capabilities.

I'm passionate about technology innovation, robotics projects, astronomy, and travel exploration. I believe in continuous learning, test-driven development, and creating collaborative environments where teams thrive through my strengths of responsibility, analytical thinking, and discipline.
